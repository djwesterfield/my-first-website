Founded in Los Angeles, CA, Rock Steady is a premier full-service marketing and label services agency designed to break the traditional mold of music promotion. Working together with emerging and established artists, Rock Steady introduces highly tailored marketing strategies and follows them from conception to execution combining unmatched precision with fresh execution. Our 360-degree approach offers an array of services including public relations, marketing, sales, digital marketing and radio promotion all under one roof.

Spearheaded by renowned indie labels Last Gang, Paper Bag, Dine Alone, Sonic Unyon and Underground Operations, Rock Steady’s core lineup made its mark by working on projects such as Cut Copy, Death Cab For Cutie, Vampire Weekend, The Shins, Coldplay, OK Go, The Presets, Lady Gaga, De La Soul, MGMT, Bjork and NERD among others. This synergy takes flight using our experience and expertise in these individual areas to create an exchange of free flowing ideas while crafting consistent results through all-encompassing packages.
